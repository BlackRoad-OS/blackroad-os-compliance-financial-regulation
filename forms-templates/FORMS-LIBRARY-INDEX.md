### CUSTOMER ACCOUNT FORMS

#### 1. New Account Application (Individuals)
**File:** `NEW-ACCOUNT-APPLICATION-INDIVIDUAL.md`
**Purpose:** Open new customer accounts for individuals  
**Required By:** FINRA Rule 4512, SEC Customer Identification Program  
**Retention:** Life of account + 6 years

#### 2. New Account Application (Business/Entity)
**File:** `NEW-ACCOUNT-APPLICATION-ENTITY.md`
**Purpose:** Open accounts for corporations, trusts, partnerships  
**Required By:** FINRA Rule 4512, Beneficial Ownership Rule  
**Retention:** Life of account + 6 years

#### 3. Customer Agreement
**File:** `CUSTOMER-AGREEMENT.md`
**Purpose:** Master account agreement with terms and conditions  
**Required By:** FINRA Rule 4512  
**Retention:** Life of account + 6 years

#### 4. Margin Agreement
**File:** `MARGIN-AGREEMENT.md`
**Purpose:** Authorization for margin trading  
**Required By:** Regulation T

## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON FORM ERRORS

### Frequent Mistakes to Avoid

**1. Missing Signatures**
- All required signatures must be obtained
- Date signatures contemporaneously
- Principal approval within required timeframes

**2. Incomplete Information**
- All required fields must be completed
- No "N/A" unless specifically permitted
- Verify accuracy of information

**3. Wrong Form Version**
- Always use current version
- Destroy old versions to prevent use
- Train staff on form updates

**4. Retention Violations**
- Follow retention schedules strictly
- Organize forms for easy retrieval
- Regular audits of filing systems

**5. Missing Principal Approval**
- All forms requiring approval must be signed
- Within required timeframes
- By appropriately registered person
