| Aspect | Series 65 (Adviser) | Series 7 (Rep) |
|--------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Standard** | Fiduciary Duty ‚≠ê | Suitability |

‚úì **Client interests come FIRST** - Always, not just sometimes
‚úì **Full disclosure** - All conflicts of interest, all fees
‚úì **Ongoing duty** - Not just at point of sale

‚úì **No self-dealing** - Cannot profit at client expense
