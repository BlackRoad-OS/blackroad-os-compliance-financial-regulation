## ğŸ”‘ Key Compliance Distinctions

### Suitability vs. Fiduciary Standard

| Aspect | Suitability (Series 7) | Fiduciary (Series 65/66) |
|--------|------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Standard** | Suitable at time of transaction | Best interest ongoing obligation |
| **Duty** | Fair dealing | Duty of care + duty of loyalty |
| **Compensation** | Commissions (transaction-based) | Fees (assets under management) |
| **Disclosure** | Conflicts disclosed | Conflicts eliminated or disclosed |
| **Relationship** | Transaction-focused | Relationship-focused |
| **Regulation** | FINRA Suitability Rule | Investment Advisers Act + State law |

### Registration Paths

| License Combination | Allowed Activities |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| **SIE + Series 7** | Sell securities (retail) |
| **Series 7 + 24** | Sell securities + supervise representatives |
| **Series 65** | Provide investment advice only (no selling) |
| **Series 7 + 63** | Sell securities in specific state |
| **Series 7 + 66** | Sell securities + provide advice (state law) |
| **SIE + Series 79** | Investment banking activities |
