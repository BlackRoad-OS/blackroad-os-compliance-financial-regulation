### 3. CONTINUING EDUCATION (CE) REQUIREMENTS

#### **FINRA Continuing Education**

**Regulatory Element:**
```
Who: All registered persons
When: 
- 2nd registration anniversary
- Every 3 years thereafter
- Within 120 days of anniversary date

Content: Computer-based training administered by FINRA
- Regulatory updates
- Ethics
- Sales practices
- Supervision

Process:
1. FINRA sends notification 60 days before due
2. Schedule with Prometric testing center
3. Complete within 120-day window
4. Pass/Fail (can retake)
5. Failure to complete = registration inactive

Firm Responsibilities:
- Track all due dates (calendar reminders)
- Remind reps 90, 60, 30 days before
- Monitor completion status in CRD
- Report completions in annual compliance report
```

**Firm Element:**
```
Who: All registered persons
When: Annually by December 31
Content: Firm-designed training (minimum requirements)

Required Topics:
- Regulatory changes affecting firm
- Products and services offered
- Suitability and supervision
- Firm policies and procedures

Delivery:
- In-person seminars
- Online modules
- Webinars
- Self-study with testing

Documentation Required:
- Training materials
- Attendance records
- Test scores
- Completion certificates

Retention: 3 years

Firm Process:
1. January: Identify training needs based on prior year
2. February: Develop training materials
3. March-November: Deliver training sessions
4. December: Verify 100% completion
5. Annual compliance report documentation
```

```

- Duration: 50 hours study
- Exam: 100 questions, 2.5 hours, 73% passing
```
